# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

SkynetBench is a research project dedicated to understanding AI safety. We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who participates, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and the research
- Show empathy toward other community members

### Research-Specific Standards

- Engage with AI safety findings in good faith, regardless of which models or providers are implicated
- Distinguish between criticism of AI system behavior and criticism of the people who build them
- Do not weaponize evaluation results against specific organizations without appropriate scientific rigor
- Do not misrepresent or selectively quote benchmark results

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional research setting
- Misrepresenting benchmark findings to defame specific AI providers

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer via GitHub private message to [@tooury](https://github.com/tooury). All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any other forums associated with SkynetBench.
